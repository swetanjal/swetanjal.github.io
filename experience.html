My Internships
I spent the summer of 2017 as an Intern at CodeChef.
My internship at CodeChef required me to teach computer programming to young kids of class 8 and 9. The aim was to inspire these students at an early age and help them prepare for the International Olympiad in Informatics(IOI). In India, students have very less exposure to programming at school level which prevents them from performing well at IOI. CodeChef aims to do away with this. I also had to design curriculum and resources(according to the IOI syllabus) which the students can use for preparation.
